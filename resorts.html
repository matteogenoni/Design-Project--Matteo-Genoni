BEST MOUNTAINS AND RESORTS



Skiing is becoming more and more of a popular sport. It started out as a sport for mountaineers, but is now more inclusive for skiers of all levels. Nowadays, ski mountains know how to attract the public by adding excellent hotels, restaurants, resorts, apres-ski entertainment, etc. But of course the most important part are the slopes themselves. The best ski resorts will always be in Europe and North America, as it is there that skiing is more popular, but other places in the world are developing more and more resorts. This is a list of the top 10 best ski resorts in the world:


1. Whistler Blackcomb, Canada

Whistler Blackcomb is the biggest ski resort in North America, including 2 mountains, more than 200 runs, and 37 lifts.

2. Courchevel, France

Courchevel is meant for more experienced skiers and offers 150 kilometers of Alpine ski terrain accessed by 60 lifts.

3. Zermatt, Switzerland

This ski area is one of the most beautiful places to ski ever. It has the greatest vertical drop and is the highest in the alps. Not to mention the Matterhorn can be seen from all around the mountain.

4. Vail Mountain Resort, Colorado, USA

This resort offers great terrain for skiers of all levels. It also has incredible hotels and restaurants, allowing incredible apres-ski entertainment.

5. Aspen Snowmass, Colorado, USA

It is made up of four ski areas and is mainly for intermediate or expert skiers.

6. Val d'Isere, France

Val d'Isere offers 300 kilometers of ski terrain served by 150 lifts.

7. Cortina D'Ampezzo, Italy

It includes 5 peaks and gives it the best setting of any other Italian ski resort.

8. Chamonix, France

Mont Blanc has the best snow conditions in the Alps due to it having Europe's tallest peak at 4,807 meters.

9. St. Anton, Austria

St. Anton hosted the first ski club in the Alps in 1901. It has a total of 280 kilometers of terrain at heights reaching 2,800 meters. It is a resort meant only for expert skiers due to its difficult runs.

10. St. Moritz, Switzerland

Last but definitely not least, St-Moritz is the most glamourous and spectacular ski resorts in Europe. It is said to be the first ski resort in Europe and has the best all round ski terrain.
