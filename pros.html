PROFESSIONAL SKIERS



Skiing has evolved over time, and so have skiers. However there are some skiers that made a mark in history, and this page will list 5 of the greatest skiers of all time.



Bode Miller, USA

Olympic and World Champion, a two-time overall World Cup champion, and most importantly, the most successful male American ski racer of all-time.

Ingemar Stenmark, Sweden

Former World Cup ski racer, he is regarded as the greatest slalom and giant slalom skier of all time with a total of 86 wins in international races.

Lindsey Vonn, USA

American World Cup Alpine ski racer, who has won four World Cup overall championships, a gold medal for downhill, and in 2016 won her 20th World Cup crystal globe title, passing Ingemar Stenmark with 19.

Hermann Maier, Austria

54 World Cup race victories (24 super-G, 15 downhills, 14 giant slaloms, and 1 combined). He is second for the men's all time list. As you can see in the picture above, he had an incredible fall during an olympic race, but he somehow managed to make an incredible recovery and compete in the next race.

Jean-Claude Killy, France

Triple Olympic champion in 1968, one of the first really successful skier, and winner of the first two World Cup titles.
